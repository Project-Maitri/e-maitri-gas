हमारे बारे में (About Us)

ई-मैत्री: डिजिटल ग्राम स्वराज की ओर एक कदम।

Our Mission: empowering rural communities with digital tools.

E-Maitri is a platform dedicated to bringing digital sovereignty to every village. We believe in the power of local governance and community-led development.


Our services bridge the gap between government schemes and the common citizen, ensuring that benefits reach those who need them most through our dedicated 'Lok Mitras'.

Vision (दृष्टिकोण)

To create a transparent and efficient local governance ecosystem.

Values (मूल्य)

Trust, Transparency, and Community Empowerment.
